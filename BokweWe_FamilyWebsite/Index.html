Our History

Where We Come From

The Bokwei We family traces its roots to Nungua, one of the traditional Ga towns along the coast of Greater Accra, Ghana. We are a people shaped by the Atlantic, by the rhythms of Ga tradition, and by the bonds of family that have held firm across generations.

Origins
Roots in Nungua
The story of the Bokwei We family begins here — in the coastal town of Nungua, among the Ga people of Greater Accra. This is where our ancestors lived, fished, and built the foundation we stand on today.
Heritage
The Nii Tetteh Santeo Gate
Through the lineage of Nii Tetteh Santeo, our branch of Bokwei We was established. This gate carries a name and a legacy — a thread of identity that connects every member of our family.
Growth
Expanding Branches
From Nungua, our family has spread across Accra, Ghana, and the world — while keeping the thread of Ga identity and Bokwei We kinship alive wherever we go.
